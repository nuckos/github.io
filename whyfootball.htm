Why? Games Football

Here is the schedule for the Football event:

  • When:   11:30 AM - after we arrive from the golf course
  • Who:    4 Four man teams
  • What:   Single elimination tournament
Rules and Regulations:
  1. 3 Touchdowns wins
  2. Flip at coin to decide who gets ball first
  3. 2 hand touch
  4. Team 1 plays 2, Team 3 plays 4
  5. 5 Mississippi
  6. 3 completes = first down
  7. 1 blitz per possession
  8. no pushing, clipping, fighting, cheating
  9. Single Elimination Tournament
  10. Losers play consolation game
  11. Pace yourself - it's a long day
  12. Don't get hurt
  13. Look out for Chin - he is huge!
  14. See Chin, Sully, Doc, or Charlie if you have any questions
  15. Good Luck
