Why? Games Golf

Here is the schedule for the Golf event:
  • Where: Scotch Hills County Club - Scotch Plains NJ - 9 Hole Course
  • When:   7:30 AM sharp - This course gets crowded
  • Who:    Playing assignments will be provided at Tee Time
  • What:   We have 4 Tee Times reserved
Rules and Regulations:
  1. You must have your own set of clubs and golf balls
  2. Maximum score per hole is double par - maximum of 8 for a par 4; maximum of 6 for a par 3; pick up ball upon reaching the max
  3. Winter rules apply - you can move the ball with your club only to improve your lie!
  4. You cannot pick up the ball
  5. You cannot move the ball from behind something without taking a penalty stroke
  6. No carts - this is a test of endurance
  7. Team score is the total score of all 4 team members
  8. One member from each of the 4 teams will make up the 4 foursomes
  9. No Mulligans!!!
  10. Balls hit into the woods from the Second Tee, will be played from the top of the hill with a 1 stroke penalty!
  11. Closest to the pin on the last hole (9th) will reward team with an extra 1/2 point (tie breaker!)
  12. Hole in one is extra 3 points for the team!
  13. No lying, cussin' or cheating!
  14. Soft spikes or sneakers recommended
  15. No drinking until after Golf
  16. See Chin, Sully, Doc, or Charlie if you have any questions
  17. Good Luck
The second event is the 4 on 4 Soccer . This will be held out at Chin's house (Beast Castle). After Golf, we have to drive out to Three Bidges. Car pooling is recommended.
