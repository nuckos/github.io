Why? Games Pass, Punt & Kick

Here is the schedule for the Pass, Punt and Kick event:
  • Where: Chin's Backyard - Three Bridges, NJ
  • When:   2:30 PM or when we arrive after soccer
  • Who:    Player line-up assignments will be provided at Pass Time
  • What:   Pass, Punt and Kick Football for total yardage
Rules and Regulations:
  1. Each player gets 2 attempts at each event
  2. No warm ups
  3. Same groupings as golf
  4. Pass twice, rest of group goes, punt twice, rest of group, kick twice
  5. Minimum score of 10 yards
  6. Distance downfield is measured from yard lines
  7. Only the highest attempt counts
  8. Total yardage is tracked
  9. No out of bounds
  10. Yardage measured downfield (so accuracy counts) not from starting point
  11. Team score is the total yardage from all four players
  12. See Chin, Sully, Doc, or Charlie if you have any questions
  13. Good Luck
