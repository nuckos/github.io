Why? Games Volleyball

Here is the schedule for the Volleyball event:
  • Where: Chin's Backyard - Three Bridges, NJ
  • When:   4 pm or whenever the Tug of War is finished
  • Who: 4 Four man teams
  • What: Double elimination tournament
Rules and Regulations:
  1. Games to 7 (by 1); win by 2; or 11 straight
  2. Team 1 plays 3, Team 2 plays 4
  3. Double elimination
  4. Only serving team can score
  5. Make your own calls (don't be a wimp or a dick)
  6. Losers move to 'losers bracket'
  7. No touching the net
  8. Don't cheat
  9. Don't get hurt
  10. See Chin, Sully, Doc, or Charlie if you have any questions
  11. Good Luck
The final event is the 2 man Horseshoes. This is the last event, also at Chin's
