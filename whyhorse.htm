Why? Games Horseshoes

Here is the schedule for the Horseshoes:
  • Where: Chin's Horseshoe Pit - Three Bridges NJ
  • When:   About 6 pm - give or take 2 hours
  • Who:    Playing assignments will be provided at Shoe Time
  • What:   2 on 2 / divide your team into 2 teams
  • Read rules below carefully! no leaners, throw 2 shoes, it all official!
Rules and Regulations:
  1. Single elimination tournament
  2. Games to 11; win by 2; or 15 straight
  3. Team 2 plays 3, Team 1 plays 4
  4. Split teammates never play each other until the final game
  5. Each player pitches both shoes followed by the opponent's two shoes.
  6. In pitching a shoe, the player may not cross the foul line.
  7. When playing teams, half the team throws from one stake and half throws from the other.
  8. Any shoe must be within one horseshoe-width (measured across the outside of the open end of the shoe) of the stake to be considered for points. (Official rules call for 6 inches max).
  9. Shoes hitting the grass or wood (logs) are 'dead' - and do not count (if they should rebound or roll into scoring position)!
  10. Shoes must land in the sand!
  11. The closest shoe to the stake gets 1 point.
  12. If you have two shoes closer than any of your opponent's, you get 2 points.
  13. Ringers are worth 3 points each and must completely encircle the stake so the ends can be touched with a straight-edge without touching the stake.
  14. If you have the closest shoe and a ringer, it's 4 points.
  15. If your opponent throws a ringer on top of yours, they cancel and no points are scored.
  16. Leaners are worth 1 point and are considered closer than any adjacent shoe except ringers. (Official rules give no value to leaners!!!).
  17. Beer Drinking and barbecue will continue
  18. See Chin, Sully, Doc, or Charlie if you have any questions
  19. Good Luck
This is the last event! Winners will be declared in the ceremony following the horseshoe finals.
