November 24, 2025 Firebirds Wood Fired Grill - Fredericksburg, VA Perfect Bullet Rye Manhattan $17.50 Jim Watson A Perfect Bullet Rye Manhattan with standard ingredients. Serving this drink in a standard chilled martini glass enhanced the drink and added to the ambiance of the modern bar with its wall of liquor that can be seen in the web page photos. A welcome respite for a weary traveller.
October 25, 2025 Circa 1886 Restaurant - Charleston, SC Perfect Bullet Rye Manhattan $19.00 Jim Watson A Classic Manhattan (not perfect) made with Michter's Rye Whiskey and Carpano Antica Vermouth. Served in a Nick and Nora glass it was the perfect match to appetizers in the upscale restaurant. It was so delicious that I may have consumed two. Garnished with 3 high quality infused cherries.
October 25, 2025 SNOB (Slightly North of Broad) Restaurant - Charleston, SC Manhattan After Dark $19.00 Jim Watson A specialty cocktail weirdly titled "Manhattan After Dark," from a high-end establishment only serving breakfast and lunch. Hence the exorbitant price and small portion. Made with Fig-infused rye, pine liquor, Japanese red wine, molasses bitters and amaro. The libation was served in a coupe glass and garnished with a small fig. Not your standard ingredients, but the taste was amazing. A wonderful start to a long and expensive day of consuming both beverage and food. While the ingredients were very exotic, the portion was nowhere near the generous pour received at the House of Bachi located in Normandy Bach, NJ.
June 7, 2025 Stracci - Alexandria, VA Elijah Craig Manhattan $15 Joe Bachi The Trastevere: Elijah Craig straight rye, fernet-branca, dolin blanc vermouth, angostura bitters. A twist on the traditional Manhattan. An excellent drink to sip on as we wait for our table at Stracci.

June 20, 2023 The Harold - Manhattan, NY Bulleit Perfect Manhattan $18 Joe and Amanda Well balanced Perfect Manhattan that was perfectly stirred. Not too sweet, not too dry, and just the right amount of bitters. The maraschino cherry was the cherry atop a delicious, albeit overpriced, Perfect Manhattan. Mismatched with lackluster calamari, resulting in an unsatisfactory combination.
June 19, 2023 Redstone - Marlton, NJ Woodford Reserve Redstone Select Manhattan $17 Chuck Bachi This custom drink is made with a unique single barrel bourbon only available at Redstone, which is why I decided to order one on a Monday night during a work meeting. My coworkers may have been surprised by my drink choice but no matter. The drink itself was full bodied, smooth and rich. The cocktail was perfectly balanced with a hint of sweetness from the vermouth and spice from the bitters with subtle cherry flavors peaking through. This Manhattan was more than just a drink, it was an experience. The first sip of the Woodford Reserve bourbon sent a surge of euphoria through my body, making me feel relaxed and happy. It was a heavenly drink that made me forget all my worries. Would order again.
