Here are real Manhattan reviews from bars & restaurants around the world

Date Bar / Restaurant Drink Price Reviewer Comments
November 24, 2025 Firebirds Wood Fired Grill – Fredericksburg, VA Perfect Bulleit Rye Manhattan $17.50 Jim Watson A Perfect Bulleit Rye Manhattan with standard ingredients. Serving this drink in a standard chilled martini glass enhanced the drink and added to the ambiance of the modern bar with its wall of liquor that can be seen in the web page photos. A welcome respite for a weary traveller.
October 25, 2025 Circa 1886 Restaurant – Charleston, SC Classic Michter’s Rye Manhattan $19.00 Jim Watson A Classic Manhattan (not perfect) made with Michter's Rye Whiskey and Carpano Antica Vermouth. Served in a Nick and Nora glass it was the perfect match to appetizers in the upscale restaurant. It was so delicious that I may have consumed two. Garnished with 3 high quality infused cherries.
October 25, 2025 SNOB (Slightly North of Broad) – Charleston, SC Manhattan After Dark $27.00 Jim Watson A specialty cocktail weirdly titled "Manhattan After Dark," from a high-end establishment only serving breakfast and lunch. Hence the exorbitant price and small portion. Made with Fig-infused rye, pine liquor, Japanese red wine, molasses bitters and amaro. The libation was served in a coupe glass and garnished with a small fig. Not your standard ingredients, but the taste was amazing. A wonderful start to a long and expensive day of consuming both beverage and food. While the ingredients were very exotic, the portion was nowhere near the generous pour received at the House of Bachi located in Normandy Beach, NJ.
April 12, 2025 Frankie's – Point Pleasant Beach, NJ Perfect Manhattan $14.25 Joe Bachi Perfect bourbon Manhattan (Bulleit?) - on the strong side, light on vermouth, but just what I was looking for on a cold rainy day. Looked so good - Barrett made multiple attempts to grab and drink it. Enjoyed one with pretzel appetizer and one with chicken Marsala special. 8.1/10
